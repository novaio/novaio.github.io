Social Media Simplified

SocialNova helps you manage and optimize your social media. Our simple interface makes managing your social media accounts easier than ever before.

The Best Way to Keep Up with Social Media.

With SocialNova, you can effectively manage your Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram accounts through a single interface.

Timeline.

SocialNova enables you to interact with the people you're following on Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook. We do this for you by combining your feeds, displaying them to you in a single feed, and enabling you to comment, like, and reply to posts made by those you follow. Simple and intuitive design; that's what we live for.

Crossposting.

You can submit a status, tweet, picture, and/or video through the SocialNova Post feature. You can post to each social media one at a time, or cross post across any combination of them simultaneously instantly. It's really that simple!

Notifications.

SocialNova aggregates all your notifications. Whether someone's mentioning your account in a comment or post, or someone's interacting with your account with a comment, like, share, or retweet, SocialNova will make sure you are notified. All your messages from your accounts get redirected to SocialNova so it's even easier to reply to them within seconds.

Scheduler.

You can use the SocialNova Scheduler to preload posts for specific times and dates. When the time to post arrives, you can release a preloaded post with one click. Over time, the SocialNova Scheduler will begin to make posting time recommendations based on total engagement. SocialNova will help you optimize your social media posting times.

Analytics.

With SocialNova Analytics, you can track the progress and growth of your social media accounts. Get insights about your follower base and discover what content resonates best with your audience. With Nova Analytics, identify your top performing posts so you can be sure that a post will perform well before spending media dollars.
