FEATURES

Our features provide you with a seamless experience.

Your Home. Your Timeline.

Nova enables you to interact with the people your following on Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook. We do this for you by combining your feeds, displaying them to you in a single feed, and enabling you to comment, like, and reply to posts made by those you follow.

One Click. All Done.

You can submit a status, tweet, picture, and/or video through the Nova Post feature. You can post to each social media one at a time, or cross post across any combination of them simultaneously.

One Place. Every Notification.

Nova aggregates all your notifications. Whether someone's mentioning your account in a comment or post, or someone's interacting with your account with a comment, like, share, or retweet, Nova will make sure your notified.

Schedule Posts. Any Time.

You can use the Nova Scheduler to preload posts for specific times and dates. When the time to post arrives, you can release a preloaded post with one click. Over time, the Nova Scheduler will begin to make posting time recommendations based on total engagement. Nova will help you optimize your social media posting times.

Our Analysis. Your Growth.

With Nova Analytics you can track the progress and growth of your social media accounts. Get insights about your follower base and discover what content resonates best with your audience. With Nova Analytics identifying your top performing posts, you can be sure that a post will perform well before spending media dollars.
